About Me

My name is Myungyun Chung, and I often go by Chris. I am currently a Junior at the University of Michigan pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I love facing complex challenges and the process of creating different solutions in order to overcome them. Softwware development incorporates both these challenges and my passion in bringing new ideas to life.

I am a full-stack developer with additional experience in both web and mobile development. No matter the task, I make sure to give maximum effort in order to learn, teach, and succeed. I greatly enjoy coding for hours in the 'zone,' as well as the collaboration of people from multiple cultural backgrounds in order to achieve a goal.

In my free time, I enjoy creating new, innovative, and enjoyable applications, working out in the gym, breakdancing, playing guitar, and watching movies. No matter what I do, I make sure to do it all for the glory of God.
